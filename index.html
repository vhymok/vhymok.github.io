About

I'm a 3rd-year Electrical Engineering student at Georgia Tech with a passion for bridging hardware and cutting-edge research. From simulating RF energy harvesters to wiring liquid rocket engines for hotfires, I thrive at the intersection of precision engineering and hands-on building.
